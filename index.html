Our Story

More Than Just Fabric

👕

We Started with a Simple Idea

Threadline was born from a frustration — it was too hard to get quality custom apparel without dealing with minimums, long waits, or uninspired designs. We built a better way.

Every order is treated like it matters, because it does. Whether you're outfitting a team, launching a brand, or just want something that's truly yours — we're here for it.

🎨

Designed to Be Worn, Not Stored

We obsess over quality. Every blank is carefully selected for feel, durability, and printability. Our printing process uses industry-leading techniques that hold up wash after wash.

Use our mockup tool to see your design before it's ever printed. What you see is what you get — no surprises.
